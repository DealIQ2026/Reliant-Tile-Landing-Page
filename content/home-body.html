OUR STORY

Tradition Built Over Generations

Father and son looking over plans at the office, circa 1990.

Reliant Tile’s story goes back to the 1960s, when my grandfather began laying tile. He was known for hard work and dedication, spending nearly his entire life in the trade and building a reputation around showing up, working hard, and doing the job right. That same standard has been passed down through our family for generations.

My father was raised around the business, spending time on job sites and learning the trade firsthand. Over time, he followed the same path, working alongside my grandfather before eventually carrying the business forward himself. Years later, I found myself in a similar place — around the job sites, learning the standards that had been passed down before me.

Today, Reliant Tile continues as a father-and-son duo, shaped by the same work ethic and attention to detail that started it all.
